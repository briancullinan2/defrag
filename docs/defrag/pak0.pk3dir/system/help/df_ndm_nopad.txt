/df_ndm_nopad [0-2] (default: 0)

Configures the jump-pads and bounce-pads in the Tricks Mode and Fast Caps gametypes.

  0: pads enabled
  1: pads disabled
  2: contact with the pads results in instant death

NOTE: The cvar df_mode must be set to Custom Settings (value 0) for this cvar to take effect.
